Our Mission

SmartFarmer is an IoT service that has a main goal of providing data analytics of farm plots as a hardware and software service. Because this project is being deployed for the purpose of improving farm quality, it takes in consideration of public health, safety, and welfare by allowing farmers to monitor their crops by aiding in tackling food insecurity.

Because the goal of this project is to improve the quality of the farms, the SmartFarmer project is able to provide improved crop yields by giving farmers more information about the health of their farms. Data analysis on soil humidity levels, soil health, and air temperature can help inform farmers with taking the correct approach in improving their farms. This granularity in information can result in a higher yield in crops and reduce the risk of poor harvests. With urban areas like Orange County often having high percentages of food insecurity in their population, having the ability to produce more food can mitigate the food insecurity issues in such urban areas. This is achieved by allocating the increase in food towards state programs like CalFresh and SNAP.

Alleviating food insecurity has a positive effect for the public, as it improves the welfare for low income populations. This improvement in welfare and an increase in food has the likely effect of improving public health particularly towards children. While the correlation of increased food security and improved public health are still needing further research, the alleviation of food insecurity still contribute to the overall wellbeing of the local population. This can be achieved by the SmartFarmer project, a project that can contribute to tackling food insecurity by providing tools to the farmer.
